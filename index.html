Rahul Rai

1989-2018
Tribal Rain

Biography

Rahul rai was born on the 14th october in 1989 in Kolkata to Padma Rai and Nayan Kumar Rai.He was the amin vocalist of Tribal Rain band.The band was formed in 2013 by Rahul Rai, Kenneth Adhikari, Prawes Lama and Sushant Ghatani. Tribal Rain comprised of late Rahul Rai on vocals/guitars, Kenneth Adhikari on keyboard, Prawes Lama on guitars and Sushant Ghatani on cajon. They initially released a single titled—Chinta, which was followed by two other singles Bhanai and Narisawna. They then released an album titled—Roka Yo Samay in 2017, consisting of 11 tracks.
The music video of Bhanai was released on 13 February 2018. A day later on 14 February 2018, the lead vocalist Rahul Rai was found dead in an apparent suicide in his own residence in Namchi.A documentary on the life of Rahul Rai was released on 14 April 2022.
