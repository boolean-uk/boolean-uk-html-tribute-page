What is Siu Mai? Siu Mai is a Classic Chinese dim sum dish also known as Shumai, Siew Mai, or 燒賣 in Chinese. Traditionally Siu Mai is made of pork and prawn/shrimp fillings wrapped in thin yellow wonton egg wrappers and steamed in a bamboo steamer.
