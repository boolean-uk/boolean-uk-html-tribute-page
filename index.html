SNOWBOARDING

Whats so amazing about it and why do it?


THE EXERCISE

Snowboarding offers a load of cardio and muscle training. The repeated impacting and cushioning you do forces your legs and core into a nearly constant active state. This strengthens and powers you up the more you do it.


THE FREEDOM

The surf like action of snowboarding is a super liberating one. When you lean from edge to edge and gain a nice carve you feel like a elegant dancer slashing up the piste. That extends all over the mountain to whatever terrain you are riding


THE SNOW

The snow is an absolute beast. From hardpack runs to tree compressed glades to sweet sweet powder. Conquering all types of snow is a treat. Especially when its pow. Powder is awesome.


THE OUTDOORS

Ultimately its the enjoyment of being in the great outdoors. Moving around fast and precise on an entire mountain really puts things in perspectice. You get a scale for the grandness of life and the world. There you are, tiny and barely visible on the side of a monolith of a mountain just finding your own peace.
