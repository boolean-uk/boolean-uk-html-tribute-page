Tribute to Minar-e-Pakistan

Minar-e-Pakistan, located in Lahore, Pakistan, is a historic monument that holds great significance in the country's history. It was here that the Lahore Resolution was passed on March 23, 1940, which laid the foundation for the creation of Pakistan.

The tower stands at a height of 70 meters (230 feet) and is a symbol of the nation's struggle for independence. It is an iconic landmark that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Minar-e-Pakistan serves as a reminder of the sacrifices made by the people of Pakistan in their quest for freedom and is a place of reflection and national pride. Wikipedia
