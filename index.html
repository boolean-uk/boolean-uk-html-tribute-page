A TRIBUTE TO MY BELOVED AUNT
Mrs Afolake Bello
Go to Wikipedia to know more

Mrs Afolake was a devoted christian, mother, sibling and wife.
She was a very important part of our lives and we miss her daily.
If i had the opportunity to get a wish granted, it would definitely be for her to be alive and healthy.
She was a compassionate carer who cared for my grandmother till she took her last breath.
She had no daughter , but she took me as hers.
She was my mom's confidant and best friend.
My mom talks about her daily.
She was the strongest person i know.
She raised four sons alone and they all turned out well.
Very sad that we lost her but we believe someday we will all re-unite in the Lord.
