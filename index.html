Benefits

Drinking water is awesome. It doesn't taste bad at all. If you drink water, you won't die of thirst.

Here this is in an unordered list format in case anyone is interested:

  • Awesome
  • Doesn't taste bad at all
  • You won't die of thirst

Drawbacks

Water doesn't taste like the best thing ever. Plus, everyone who ever drank water ended up dying so it's highly suspicious. Also people say that humanity is running out of water so it might end up being the cause for the next World War and the inevitable destruction of humanity.

Here this is in an ordered list format in case anyone is interested:

  1. Doesn't taste great
  2. Everyone who drinks water ends up dying
  3. Potential reason for the downfall of humanity

Noteble fans

There are notable fans of water. Here is a poem about water by Samuel Taylor Coleridge, called The Rime of the Ancient Mariner.

Day after day, day after day,
We stuck, nor breath nor motion;
As idle as a painted ship
Upon a painted ocean.

Water, water, every where,
And all the boards did shrink;
Water, water, every where,
Nor any drop to drink.

The very deep did rot: O Christ!
That ever this should be!
Yea, slimy things did crawl with legs
Upon the slimy sea.

About, about, in reel and rout
The death-fires danced at night;
The water, like a witch’s oils,
Burnt green, and blue and white…
